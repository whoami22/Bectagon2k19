Technical Quiz

Round 1: Qualification Round

  • A team of maximum 2 players can register.
  • The quiz comprises of 20 questions (multiple choice and true/false).
  • Time duration for the quiz is 30-45 minutes.

Round 2: Elimination Round

  • The teams that clear Qualification Round, enters the Elimination Round.
  • The top 4 teams shall qualify for the Grand Finale.
  • The duration of the quiz will be around 30-45 minutes.

Round 3: The Grand Finale

  • A quiz will be conducted with a quiz master.
  • Quiz will be carried out in multiple rounds.

Certificate Policy

  • Cash prizes, Certificate of Excellence and momentoes will be awarded to teams in the final as per their positions.

Industry Problem Solving

Are you a research enthusiast? Are you innovative enough to make any industry process better or best? Are you a guy/girl who has an idea of making innovative things that eases our lives? Are you having any innovative idea related to your field of study? Then don’t wait, gear up your body and soul to industry problem solving, where your endeavour gets recognised and rewarded. Your idea will be executed by a group of panels who are expertise in the field of study.

Rules and Regulations

  • maximum of two members per team.
  • Participants must provide their valid Identity card.
  • Team members should register online for the event.
  • For screening purposes, a pre-event abstract in .pdf format. Abstract, of maximum 500 words, minimum 200 words, should include a brief introduction, motivation, methodology adopted, results obtained and practical application.
  • Short listed teams will be informed by mail.
  • Selected participants will be given 20 minutes to present their paper before a panel of judges, of which 15 minutes will be for presentation and 5 minutes for the discussion.
  • Any kind of plagiarism is not allowed.
  • The participants are expected to bring 3 hard copies as well as a soft copy of the paper and abstract.
  • Full paper format should be as per the following attachment: JAER_template

Problem Statement

"Dedicated continuous processes often put batch processes out of business" says the textbook "Systematic methods of chemical process design" by Biegler LT, et al. Contestants are supposed to design batch as well as continuous distillation separation of ternary mixture Methanol-toluene-water and perform cost comparison of both to establish the above. They can use softwares of their choice.

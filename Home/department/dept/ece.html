Paper Presentation

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • It consists of only ONE round.
  • The font size should be as mentioned below:-
    • For titles and sub headings the font size should be-14
    • For descriptive ones the font size should be-12
  • Members per Team -- 2
  • Number of slides it should not exceed 10
  • Timings :
    • Presentation for ppt – 5Minutes
    • Queries – 2Minutes
  • First Slide must be: Topic name, College name, Roll number and Register number.
  • In the second slide give a glimpse of the topic and then continue with brief explanation of the topic from the third slide.
  • The topics to be selected should be a technical one which is related to the branch .Ex:-Communications, Antennas, V.L.S.I, Embedded systems etc..,
  • All abstract are send to email-bectagonbecece2019@gmail.com

Tricky Circuit

Introduction:

  • In this event the participant’s knowledge on Integrated circuits and some standard electronic circuits will be tested.

Event rules:

  • InTeams of three participantscan contest in this event.
  • There will be total two rounds

Round 1:

  • This first round will be elimination round with objective questions from the fields Electronics and Communications (Separate question paper will be given for each and every year).
  • Written test in this round consists of 20 questions.
  • Each question carries one mark.
  • Total test time is 20 minutes.

Round 2:

  • In the second round questions related to circuits and ICs will be asked every team (With eachright answer TWO mark will be added to the total score.
  • For every wrong answer 0.5marks will be deducted from the total score).
  • For answering each question Three minutes time will be allotted.
  • If any batch does not answer their question chance will be given to the next batch.
  • Depending on the overall score the winners will be announced.

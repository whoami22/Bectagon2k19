Paper Presentation

Topics

  • Applications of AI techniques to power system
  • Wireless power transmission with GPS interface
  • Renewable energy sources
  • Irrigation control system
  • Smart Grid-future electric grid
  • Energy applications of Nano technology
  • Substation Automation communication protocol
  • Micro fuel cells
  • Future trends in power system control
  • Power theft detection techniques
  • Modern trends in machine design technology
  • Power quality issues and mitigation techniques
  • Any advanced topics inelectrical systems

Rules:

  • Candidates should send their paper in soft copy to our mail id.
  • Accepted papers will be intimated through their respective mail.id
  • The presentation time is for 8 mins and queries for 2 mins for each paper.
  • Selected candidates should bring their identity card at the time of presentation.
  • Selected candidates should bring two hard copies and one soft copy of the paper at the time of presentation (MS Office 2003, 2007 format).
  • Only LCD projectors will be provided.
  • Two copies of abstract, not exceeding 300 words.
  • Spot entries are also allowed.

Paper Submission Rules:

  • .Working Models, Samples Fetch You Extra Points.
  • Participants should submit the soft copy of the full paper in IEEE format (abstract alone will not be accepted) and email.
  • Paper should be submitted as a MS word document (.doc) or pdf document.
  • Bring your Soft Copies in CD or Pen drive.
  • Paper should not exceed eight pages.
  • Details of the team members: Name, Department, E-Mail ID, Contact number, Institution name should be sent along with the Paper.
  • Send soft copy to :eeebectagon@gmail.com

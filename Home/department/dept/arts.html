Theme Painting

RULES:

  • Item will be conducted on the spot and participant will be requested to do painting on the subject given by the In-charge (s) of the competition.
  • Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  • Size of the painting will be half imperial size drawing paper i.e. 22 inches x 15 inches
  • Painting can be done in oil, water, poster or pastel colours.
  • Candidate shall bring their own material like brushes, paints etc. only the paper / sheet will be provided.

Cartooning

Rangoli

RULES:

  • Duration will not be more than 2 hours
  • Participant shall bring his/her own material. This art is know differently in various regions such as Mandna, Alpana, Alekhan , Kolam, Rangoli, etc. For this the medium and form for expression can be free hand, pictorial and descriptive. Only one medium shall be used – Poster Colours or Petals or Saw – dust or Pulses or Rice without pasting.
  • The participants shall have to prepare a rangoli within the space provided by the organizer on the given theme.
  • SALT SHOULD NOT BE USED

Face Painting

RULES:

  • Duration will not be more than 2 hours
  • Participant must bring their own material.
  • Participant must bring their pair for face painting.
  • Judges decision shall be final.

Diya Painting

RULES:

  • Duration will not be more than 1 hour
  • Diya will be provided. Participants must bring their own paintings and brushes.
  • Judgement will be based on most artistic painting.
  • Judges decision shall be final.

Clay Modelling

RULES:

  • Item will be conducted on the spot.
  • Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  • Topics / size and other specific rules shall be announced on the spot.
  • Clay shall NOT be provided by the host University.
